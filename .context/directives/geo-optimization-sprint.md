# Directive: GEO Optimization Sprint — Schema Markup + Content Restructuring

**Source**: Scout 2026-03-02, proposed by Priya
**Priority**: P1
**Risk**: low
**Recommended process**: design-then-build
**Goal alignment**: growth-marketing

## Objective

AI-referred traffic is growing 165x faster than organic search. 80% of LLM citations don't rank in Google's top 100 — this is an entirely separate discovery channel. Our product pages are fact-dense but lack the structured data and modular content format AI engines need to extract and cite. This is the highest-leverage SEO investment for 2026.

## Scope

Audit and enhance JSON-LD structured data across BuyWisely (priority), SellWisely, and PricesAPI. Add Product, Review, FAQ, Organization, BreadcrumbList schema to all key pages. Restructure content to front-load facts in first 30% (where 44.2% of citations come from). Ensure FCP < 0.4s (correlates with 6.7 citations). Measure citation baseline first.

## Intelligence Context

- AI traffic growing 165x faster than organic search
- 44.2% of LLM citations from first 30% of content
- Articles 2900+ words average 5.1 citations vs 3.2 for short
- FCP < 0.4s averages 6.7 citations vs fewer for slower pages
- Listicles ("Best X" articles) account for 43.8% of ChatGPT-cited page types
- 80% of LLM citations don't rank in Google top 100 — separate channel

## Success Criteria

- Product, Review, FAQ, Organization schema on all BuyWisely product pages
- All pages pass Google Rich Results Test with zero errors
- Key landing pages restructured with facts front-loaded
- FCP verified < 0.4s on all key pages
- Citation baseline measured for future comparison
