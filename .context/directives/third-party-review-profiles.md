# Directive: Create Third-Party Review Platform Profiles
**Requires**: manual

**Source**: Scout 2026-03-02, proposed by Priya + Marcus
**Priority**: P1
**Risk**: low
**Recommended process**: fix
**Goal alignment**: growth-marketing

## Objective

Sites with profiles on G2, Capterra, Trustpilot have 3x higher chance of being cited by AI systems. Brands are 6.5x more likely to be cited via third-party sources than their own domains. We currently have zero profiles on any review platform — this is the single highest-leverage marketing gap.

## Scope

Create profiles on:
- **SellWisely**: G2, Capterra, AlternativeTo, SaaSHub
- **BuyWisely**: Trustpilot, Product Review AU
- **PricesAPI**: G2, AlternativeTo

Complete all profile fields, add screenshots, feature lists, pricing info. Solicit initial reviews from beta users.

## Intelligence Context

- Wikipedia (7.8%), Reddit (1.8%), Forbes (1.1%), G2 (1.1%) lead ChatGPT citations
- 3x higher citation chance with review platform profiles
- 6.5x more likely to be cited via third-party sources than own domains
- Zero cost to create profiles, compounds over time

## Success Criteria

- All profiles created and fully populated
- Screenshots and feature lists added to each
- At least 2 initial reviews solicited per product
