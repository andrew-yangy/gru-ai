# Directive: SellWisely Competitor Comparison Pages

**Source**: Scout 2026-03-02, proposed by Marcus + Priya
**Priority**: P1
**Risk**: low
**Recommended process**: design-then-build
**Goal alignment**: sellwisely-revenue

## Objective

Comparison pages are the #1 bottom-funnel content format. None of the 8 major competitor monitoring tools claim AU specialization. Documented user pain points in Prisync/Price2Spy (scraping errors, clunky UX, hostile cancellation) provide direct copy material. Listicles account for 43.8% of ChatGPT-cited page types, making these pages dual-purpose for SEO and GEO.

## Scope

Create 3-4 comparison pages:
1. "SellWisely vs Prisync" — AU data depth, zero setup, pricing comparison
2. "SellWisely vs Price2Spy" — Accuracy (own data vs scraping), UX, transparency
3. "Best Price Monitoring Tools for Australian Retailers 2026" — Listicle format
4. "SellWisely vs Competera" — SMB-friendly vs enterprise-only

Each page with comparison tables, feature matrices, AU-specific differentiators, FAQ sections with schema markup.

## Intelligence Context

- B2B price monitoring market: $710M, projected $1.4B by 2033
- Price2Spy: scraping errors, complex UI, can't self-service unsubscribe
- Prisync: data still requires manual gathering, hostile cancellation reported
- No competitor claims AU specialization — gap in the market
- SellWisely unique advantage: pre-loaded AU data (no scraping errors), instant setup

## Success Criteria

- 3+ comparison pages published on sellwisely.io
- Each page has structured data (FAQ schema, comparison tables)
- Pages rank for "[competitor] alternative" keywords within 60 days
